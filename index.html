Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 30, 2025

1. Information We Collect

At Clockly, we take your privacy seriously. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Email addresses (when you subscribe to our newsletter)
  • Contact information (when you reach out to us)
  • Usage data (how you interact with our website)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To send you newsletters and productivity tips (with your consent)
  • To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • To improve our website and user experience
  • To detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission
  • Secure servers and databases
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal data

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies help us:

  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Provide personalized content

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

We may use third-party services for:

  • Payment processing (for premium features)

These services have their own privacy policies.

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data correction or deletion
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time
  • Opt-out of data collection
